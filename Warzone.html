War Zone is a competitive game mode where players battle endless hordes of Corrupted for ranked scoring and War Zone Influence. In War Zone, Commandants - meaning the player, deploy teams of their choice in two of five available elementally-themed regions. Each region possesses three stages for the user to clear; clearing these stages will earn the user a score. There are five War Zone regions in total. During each cycle, two regions will randomly become available for the user to challenge. Each of the five regions has different elemental properties
